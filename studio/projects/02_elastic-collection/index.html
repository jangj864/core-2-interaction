The title of '295HOURS', is Total amount of hours I watched movies in theater as a Cinephile.
The data on this page contains my personal information from 2018 to 2023.

A white font on a black background was used,
a monochromatic color that gives the feeling of the ending credits of a movie theater.
The analysis content of this web page is largely divided into two. One is a circular chart that allows you to compare the total amount at a specific time according to several sections and categories, and the other is a table that sorts all data through the desired filter. You can look at the content.

Reasonable experiences are shown in green, and regrettable experiences are shown in purple.
